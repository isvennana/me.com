Featured Case Study

B2B Product Marketing and Search Intent Strategy Portfolio | DY Engineering

Product category opportunity · Customer problem language · Narrow CEP design · B2B demand generation

This case began as a Naver Search Ads performance issue, but the deeper challenge was product-category positioning: how to make complex B2B industrial products discoverable through customer problem language, narrow Category Entry Points, and product-fit signals rather than broad, high-cost generic keywords.

Problem Definition

Broad product keywords created cost, but product-fit intent created value. A click did not equal revenue; a meaningful conversion was closer to a phone inquiry, quotation request, production-feasibility question, or project-fit consultation.

Strategy

Excluded overly broad competitive positions and built long-tail keyword clusters around product fit, customer problem situations, technical structure, installation conditions, and consultation-oriented conversion.
